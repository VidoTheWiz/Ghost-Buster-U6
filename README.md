Ghost Buster\
Kill as many ghosts as you can before they take the newly departed souls, or rather, the green orbs.\
The platform chosen for this game is MR.\
Shoot them with your ray gun, and they'll disappear, but if they get the souls(orbs), it's over. It's a free run until you can't anymore.\
The interactive objects are the ray gun, the ghosts, and the UI button. The ghosts use a navmesh of the passthrough space to arrive at the orbs.\
